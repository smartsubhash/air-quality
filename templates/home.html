About Our Air Quality Monitor

Our platform helps you understand and monitor air pollution in your environment. By taking our survey, you contribute to a growing database of air quality information that helps researchers identify pollution trends and develop solutions.

Benefits of using our platform:

  • Get personalized air quality assessments
  • Contribute to environmental research
  • Track pollution changes over time
  • Receive recommendations for reducing exposure
